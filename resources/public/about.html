About

The reason I love Beats Music above Rdio and the rest is the curation and "sentence" features. The problem is that the sentence feature is unavailable while on a plane and that sucks.

Luckily there is an API! I created this app to take a tracks from the sentence feature and write them to a playlist, which can then be set for offline playback.

If you have any questions about the app, you can reach out to me on Twitter (@mikeflynn_) and you can even view the source on GitHub.
